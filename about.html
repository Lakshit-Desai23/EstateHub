Our Story

Founded in 2011, EstateHub started with a simple mission: make real estate accessible, transparent, and stress-free for everyone. What began as a small team of five agents in New York has grown into a nationwide network of over 120 licensed professionals.

We believe that finding a home should be an exciting journey, not an overwhelming ordeal. That's why we combine cutting-edge technology with genuine human expertise to guide every client from search to closing.

Today, we've helped over 3,200 families buy, sell, and rent properties across the United States, and we're just getting started.
